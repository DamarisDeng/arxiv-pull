Today's q-bio.QM papers converge on a shared preoccupation with data efficiency and principled quantification under real-world constraints — whether those constraints are the scarcity of experimentally annotated enzyme sequences, the uneven geographic distribution of antimicrobial resistance surveillance records, or the limited sensory information available to a navigating bacterium.
